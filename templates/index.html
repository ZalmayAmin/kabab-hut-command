Fifteen Years of Flame

Tradition
reimagined.

Kabab Hut is a traditional Pakistani restaurant known for high-quality and authentic halal food. We are a family-owned business for more than 15 years in the United States.

Our secret? Organic charcoal and the patience to let the flame do the work.

01

The Ember

We use only 100% organic lump charcoal. No chemicals, just pure heat that imparts a clean, unmistakable smokiness.

02

The Spice

Our spices are hand-ground weekly. We toast each seed to release the essential oils before blending them into our secret marinades.

03

The Patience

True kabab is never rushed. We let the meat rest, then grill it slowly over indirect heat until it reaches the perfect charred edge.
